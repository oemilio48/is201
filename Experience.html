What I've Done

Hi, my name is Emilio Ozuna, a pre-business major at BYU. I'm passionate about getting things done right and on time and am known for my excellent attention to detail. I'm hoping work in the field of supply chain management with an emphasis on negotiations. I want to be able to use my skills to help companies of all sizes become efficient and build bridges internationally among businesses.

While there I was able to lead groups of 16+ missionaries. Several times I was in charge of instructing these large groups of missionaries to improve the efficiency in their work. I was in charge of personally training three new missionaries who in turn each went on to train several others

I was born and raised in Denver. My parents are from Mexico which has allowed me to become bilingual. Being out in nature is one of my favorite passtimes.
